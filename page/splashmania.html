About

Where nature meets fun, and where you can spend the entire day splashing around under the sun! Experience the thrills and spills of a one-of-a-kind waterpark in SplashMania, Asia’s Largest Rainforest Themed Waterpark.

Operating Hours

Home to 39 exhilarating slides and attractions that cater to families, students, young working adults and thrill seekers of all kinds, this waterpark is located in Gamuda Cove, Selangor. Within close proximity to KLIA and surrounding cities, SplashMania is slated to become an international destination once its doors open.
